About the Waukegan Symphony Orchestra

Founded in 1974, the Waukegan Symphony Orchestra has been a cornerstone of the Lake County arts community. Our orchestra is composed of professional and amateur musicians, all dedicated to making world-class music accessible to everyone.

We perform several concerts each year, featuring a wide variety of repertoire from Beethoven to modern composers. Join us for our next performance and feel the magic of live symphonic music.
